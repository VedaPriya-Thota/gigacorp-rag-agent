GIGACORP CUSTOMER SUPPORT FAQ

=== SHIPPING POLICY ===
GigaCorp ships to over 40 countries worldwide, including the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Germany, and India.
Standard domestic shipping (within the United States) takes 3-5 business days and costs $4.99, or is free on orders over $50.
International shipping, including to India, typically takes 7-14 business days.
The shipping cost to India is a flat rate of $12.99 for orders under $75, and free for orders of $75 or more.
Express international shipping is available for an additional $25 and reduces delivery time to 3-5 business days.
All orders receive a tracking number via email within 24 hours of dispatch.
GigaCorp is not responsible for customs duties or import taxes, which are the buyer's responsibility in the destination country.

=== RETURN POLICY ===
GigaCorp accepts returns within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund.
Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by the original receipt or order confirmation.
To initiate a return, customers should log into their GigaCorp account, navigate to "Order History," and select "Request Return."
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the returned item is received at our warehouse.
Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items; otherwise, a $5.99 return shipping fee applies.
Final sale items, marked clearly at checkout, are not eligible for return or exchange.
Exchanges for a different size or color can be requested within 45 days of delivery.

=== BUSINESS HOURS ===
GigaCorp customer support is available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM EST.
Live chat support is available 24/7 through the GigaCorp website.
Phone support is closed on all major US federal holidays.
Email inquiries submitted outside business hours receive a response within 1 business day.
Our physical retail locations are open Monday through Saturday, 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM local time, and closed on Sundays.

=== SERVICE TIERS ===
GigaCorp offers three membership tiers: Basic, Plus, and Premium.
The Basic tier is free and includes standard shipping rates and standard customer support response times.
The Plus tier costs $9.99/month and includes free standard shipping on all orders, plus priority email support with a 4-hour response time.
The Premium tier costs $19.99/month and includes free express shipping worldwide, 24/7 phone support, and an extended 60-day return window.
Premium members also receive early access to sales events, typically 48 hours before they open to the public.
Members can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel their tier at any time from the account settings page, with changes taking effect at the next billing cycle.

=== ACCOUNT & PAYMENT ===
GigaCorp accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay.
Payment information is encrypted and GigaCorp never stores full card numbers on its servers.
If a payment fails, customers have 48 hours to update their payment method before the order is automatically cancelled.
Gift cards can be purchased in denominations of $25, $50, $100, or $200 and never expire.
